Our Story

Born From Flame

Brasero began with a single obsession: the transformative power of live fire. Chef Marco Reyes spent years cooking across South America and the Basque Country, learning that the best flavour comes from patience, heat, and respect for the ingredient.

In 2019, he brought that philosophy to Shoreditch — converting a Victorian railway arch into a temple to the grill. Charcoal, oak, and cherry wood are the only flavour enhancers that matter here.

Every cut of meat is sourced from single-estate British farms. Every fish arrives from day-boats. Nothing is rushed. Everything burns slowly, beautifully, deliberately.
